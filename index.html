Fanxu Meng(孟繁续)

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Institute for Artificial Intelligence, Peking University, under the supervision of Prof. Muhan Zhang. My research focuses on efficient LLMs and model compression. I have also served as a reviewer for NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, COLM and TPAMI.

News

  • May 2025: CLOVER was accepted as a poster presentation at ICML 2025.
  • October 2024: PiSSA was accepted as a spotlight presentation (Rating 8,8,7,7) at NeurIPS 2024.
  • June 2024: Delivered invited talks on PiSSA at 青稞社区 (Video) and LOGS (Video).
  • May 2024: PiSSA was merged into the main branch of huggingface/peft.
  • April 2024: Published "PiSSA: Principal Singular Values and Singular Vectors Adaptation" on arXiv, along with its source code.
  • September 2022: Became a full-time Ph.D. student at the Institute for Artificial Intelligence, Peking University.
  • October 2021: Presented an invited talk on Efficient Networks and RMNet at 智东西.
  • January 2021: Began working full-time at Tencent Youtu Lab, collaborating with Hao Cheng, Ke Li and Xing Sun.
  • January 2021: Graduated from Harbin Institute of Technology with the Outstanding Graduate Award under the supervision of Prof. Guangming Lu.
  • October 2020: Our paper "Pruning Filter in Filter" was accepted at NeurIPS 2020.
  • March 2020: Our paper "Filter Grafting for Deep Neural Networks" was accepted at CVPR 2020.
